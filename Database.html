Database Arrangement – Online Workshop Management System

Users

  • user_id (Primary Key)
  • full_name
  • email
  • password
  • mobile
  • role (Admin / User)
  • created_at

Workshops

  • workshop_id (Primary Key)
  • title
  • description
  • date
  • time
  • trainer_name
  • status (Upcoming / Completed)

Registrations

  • registration_id (Primary Key)
  • user_id (Foreign Key)
  • workshop_id (Foreign Key)
  • registration_date
  • attendance_status

Live Sessions

  • session_id (Primary Key)
  • workshop_id (Foreign Key)
  • meeting_link
  • start_time
  • end_time

Materials

  • material_id (Primary Key)
  • workshop_id (Foreign Key)
  • material_title
  • file_url
  • upload_date

Feedback

  • feedback_id (Primary Key)
  • user_id (Foreign Key)
  • workshop_id (Foreign Key)
  • rating
  • comments
  • submitted_on
